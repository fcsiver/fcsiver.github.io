Optimize Your Nutrition, Maximize Your Life

Welcome! I am Frances Siver, a Registered Dietitian Nutritionist with a Bachelor of Science in Chemistry from the University of Florida, a Master’s of Science in Human Nutrition from Louisiana State University, and a Bachelor of Science in Nutrition and Dietetics from the University of North Florida.

I have done work as a pharmaceutical research scientist, an associate nutrition researcher, and as a graduate student at LSU. While at LSU I coauthored several papers concerning Vitamin D, and conducted research on how chromium and insulin affect the body.

Now, I use my strong scientific background, unique insights into emerging nutrition information, and dietary analytical skills to help you manage your dietary needs, relieve chronic inflammation, and develop nutritional strategies for longevity.

My specialty areas include:

• Outlining food sensitives and integrative and functional nutrition

• Nutrition Assessments for better health and optimal energy

• Assessing and resolving gastrointestinal issues (GERD, IBS, etc.)

• Anti-Aging Strategies / Longevity Strategies via Optimum Nutrition
